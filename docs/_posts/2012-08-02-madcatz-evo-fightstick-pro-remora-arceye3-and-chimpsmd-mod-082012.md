My son and I went to [EVO](http://evo.shoryuken.com/) again this year and in addition to spending way too much money in general, I bought the [MadCatz EVO limited edition FightStick Pro](http://shoryuken.com/2012/06/26/official-evo-fightstick-from-madcatz-on-sale-evo-weekend/) for myself. The clear Madcatz limited edition EVO 2012 FightStick Pro is a thing of beauty to begin with. But it is just begging to be modded. Of course, I had to dual-console-mod it so I could use it in the XBox360 and PS3. But I also had to add some Button LEDs while I was at it. The new ArcEye3's, plus the new Remora board, plus a blue ball stick top and blue shaft and blue translucent Seimitsu buttons all combine to be the most beautiful arcade stick I've ever seen.
