Just had to publicly give the guys at [newegg.com](http://newegg.com) praise.

I ordered 1 Gig of Laptop RAM (DDR333, PC2700) for my brand new, shiny, pretty powerbook. First off, they had a great price--$109 for Kingston. Secondly, they have an amazing level of service--very timely, informative e-mails letting me know how my order is doing. Third, I placed my order on the night of the 15th at 11:30 PM. I just got my RAM delivered to me today--basically one day later.

Wow.

Color me VERY impressed. I'll definitely be back.

Nicely done, [newegg.com](http://newegg.com)! I heartily recommend them.
