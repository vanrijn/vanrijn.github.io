My workmate, Mike, came in to work singing (well, okay, the song has no words, so he was humming it) this song that I immediately remembered but couldn't for the life of me figure out what it was. And the worst part was that since the song has no words, I didn't think I'd be able to google it enough to figure out what it was.

Little did I know that google is mightier than the spoken word. I underestimated the power of the google, I admit it.

I typed "banamana" into google and before I knew it, I found [this page](http://tdaxp.blogspirit.com/archive/2005/03/03/ma-na_ma-na_song.html) that had someone else's similar story (the getting the song stuck in your head and not being able to figure out what it is). TOO FUNNY!!

Of importance, though... [here's the Muppets video of the "Mahna Mahna song"](http://video.google.com/videoplay?docid=-4668196322523357460) (see, Mike, I told you it was the Muppets!!!)

Here's a [high-quality MP3 of the glorious Mahna Mahna song](http://www.latke.net/mahnamahna/mahnamahna.mp3)!!

Here's a [wikipedia page](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mahna_Mahna) for this amazing song.

Words cannot possibly express how fantastic this song is. Genious, I say!! They've even used it recently in a Doctor Pepper commercial!
