At some point, in the most probably distant future, someone will stumble upon these ramblings and wonder what sort of maniac would leave such entries as these, mine. But for the present, as my readership exists solely of myself and Opera's refresh button, I shall conclude my Chronicles of Riddick(ulous) : The Quest for One Good Camera. That is to say that I hope this is the conclusion of said Chronicle, because I have invested enough of my time on this planet on it already.

The Kodak DX7440, combined with a free 256M PNY SD card, and a $20 coupon at my local Staples has declared itself as the most eligible bachelor. Thus far, I am very impressed with the wee beastie. It has tons of features and I've yet to see a bad picture coming from it.

I suppose that's all for now....
