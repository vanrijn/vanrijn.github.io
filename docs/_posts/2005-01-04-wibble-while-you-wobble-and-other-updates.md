So, I've discovered [BloGTK](http://blogtk.sourceforge.net/) just now, and this post here will be the maiden voyage of said tool in combination with my Wordpress blog.... (looks pretty good thus far!!! =:) )

In other news, I found [a very interesting blog today](http://www.mcgladrey-family.us/kayne/archives/2004/10/10/solving_the_fscking_problem) from which I'll quote...

> One of the joys of running Linux is that all the Windows problems - spyware, virii, worms, zombies, etc - don't apply. There's a flip side to this: when something goes wrong, it's a private hell because you can't just take your desktop to a shop.

Heh. That's a pretty profound couple of sentences, really. I think that's why some of my good friends have abandoned Linux. To clarify, I think that when you no longer are able to tolerate the once-in-a-while problems that Linux might have (most of the times they're related to wanting to do things with hardware/software that the hardware/software vendors have not allowed to work in Linux), WindowsXP starts to look mighty enticing. Things just work there, after all, right? =;) Don't fool yourself--there's 2 sides to that coin. It's your decision as to which is the lesser of 2 evils. If I had all the money in the world, I'd likely be the proud owner of 6 Macs. But I don't and I'm not. And I still value the freedom that Linux gives me over the comfortable cell that Windows gives you.

Also, I've discovered [amaroK](http://amarok.kde.org/) lately, and I'm completely taken with it. It's exactly what I've been looking for in a media player for the last 4 years or so. =:) VERY nice job guys.
