This is pretty deep and insightful for a Thursday afternoon...

> "What's fascinating to me is that I grew up in a time when my friends related to Luke and now, my kids relate to [Anakin](http://starwars.com/databank/character/anakinskywalker/). There is an interesting social comment there -- that when I grew up the hero was an optimistic young neophyte who becomes this hero and the new generation's hero is a strong-willed, ambitious and ultimately vilified protagonist who is misled and, for reasons of ego and heartbreak, literally becomes the very villain that my generation fought *against*." -- Director [JJ Abrams](http://starwars.com/community/news/rocks/f20060505/index.html)
