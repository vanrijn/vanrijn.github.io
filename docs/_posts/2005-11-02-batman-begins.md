My good friend [Mr. Schindler](http://mschindler.com) just let me borrow [Batman Begins](http://www.batmanbegins.com) from him and I took the time to watch it this evening instead of patching, sanding, painting, cleaning, organizing, and stressing. All I can say is *WOW*.

Batman Begins is easily the best Batman movie ever. It is what Tim Burton was striving for, I really believe, in [his first Batman with Michael Keaton](http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0096895/). Don't get me wrong, I liked the first one. The subsequent Hollywood offerings for the Dark Knight on the silver screen were... how shall I put it... *utter* crap, so I won't comment as to their lack of merits. But Batman Begins was absolutely brilliant in its exploration of Batman's beginnings, training, techno-arsenal, and psyche. About the only thing that I felt was lacking were the really tight fight scenes--too tight to really be able to see the action. But other than that, I have no complaints whatsoever.

My cat thought I was nuts, to be sure, but I was laughing, crying, and cheering throughout the movie.

Very, very, very well done.

Oh--and they finally did the [Batmobile](http://auto.howstuffworks.com/batmobile.htm) justice!! I LOVED it!

And now to sleep, perchance to dream... that... [I'm Batman](http://www.advertisementave.com/tv/ad.asp?adid=30).
