But for the present, as my readership exists solely of myself and Opera's refresh button, I shall conclude my Chronicles of Riddick(ulous) : The Quest for One Good Camera. That is to say that I hope this is the conclusion of said Chronicle, because I have invested enough of my time on this planet on it already.

[The Kodak DX7440](http://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/tg/detail/-/B00022AH6I/qid=1103578106/sr=8-1/ref=pd_csp_1/002-3641461-1418401?v=glance&s=photo&n=507846), combined with a free 256M PNY SD card, and a $20 coupon at my local Staples has declared itself as the most eligible bachelor. Thus far, I am very impressed with the wee beastie. It has tons of features and I've yet to see a bad picture coming from it.

I suppose that's all for now....
