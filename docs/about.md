My name is Jason 'vanRijn' Kasper.

The Jason part was given to me by my parents, though I have many times wished I had been named Richard the Third, after my Dad and Grandfather.

The Kasper part was passed down to me from my Dad, though there are some pretty interesting facts about the [family's lineage](http://www.houseofnames.com/xq/asp.familycrest_details/s.Kasper/Kasper_family_Crest/Kasper_coat_of_arms/qx/Kasper.htm).

The 'vanRijn' part is something that happened on the way between then and now, but you can [read more about that later](http://movingparts.net/index.php?p=10).

If you want to reach me, the best way to do that is to [email me at vR@movingparts.net](mailto:vR@movingparts.net).

My current title at work is Software Engineer at Apple. In human-speak, that means that I'm a nerd who can and enjoys to:

- Program computers in more than 10 different languages.
- Understand complex problems that need to be solved with computer systems and create solutions, even if that means being the first to implement a given solution. This does not mean that I enjoy or advocate re-inventing the wheel. I'm strongly against such silliness and will research existing solutions thoroughly before inventing a new one.
- Architect interacting computer systems to solve problems.
- Design the interactions, flows, abilities, security, and limitations that a system or set of systems should have.
- Work with the Open Source community and use Open Source systems and technologies.

You can find more about my work experience and other [THINGS here at LinkedIn](http://www.linkedin.com/in/nosajka).

I am a Christian.

This manifests itself in unexpected snowball fights, lengthy video-game sessions, random [Airzooka missions](http://blogs.msdn.com/tristank/archive/2004/12/07/275579.aspx), and late-night Guild Wars 2 sessions.
